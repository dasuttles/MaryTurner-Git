Upcoming Events

On May 17th, 2014 @ 11 a.m. at Webb Miller Community Church the MTP will be having its 4th Annual Mary Turner Commemoration gathering. This historic gathering will include a program of speakers, a shared meal, and a caravan out to the site of Mrs. Turner's murder for final thoughts and prayers. Please let us know if you plan to attend so we have an accurate idea of how much food we will need. To confirm/RSVP simply send an an email or give us a call at 229-234-2856.

The MTP meets every 3rd Monday of the month (5:30 p.m.) at the Department of Women and Gender Studies (Carswell Hall) on the campus of Valdosta State University.

The MTP organizes two to three major public forums each year so be sure to check back with us for newly posted upcoming events.
