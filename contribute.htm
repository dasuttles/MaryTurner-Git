Donate to the Mary Turner Project

The Mary Turner Project has a variety of ongoing projects that require resources to carry out. Some of that work includes the following.

  • Ongoing research on south Georgia's legacy of racial terrorism

  • Regular community forums and printing costs for media on race related issues

  • The annual Mary Turner Commemoration held each May

  • The maintenance and expansion of www.maryturner.org

If you would like to financially support our work it would be deeply appreciated. All contributions are tax deductible and all proceeds go to our work.
